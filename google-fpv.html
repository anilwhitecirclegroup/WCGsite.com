The Idea

To capture the essence of Google’s newest office in Bangalore, we took to the skies - literally . Using a high-speed FPV drone, we filmed every floor of the building in one seamless, cinematic flight. The drone weaved through corridors, breakout zones, open workspaces, and collaborative areas - giving viewers a first-person tour that was fast-paced, immersive, and visually striking. The final cut wasn’t just a walkthrough; it was a bold statement of Google’s culture, energy, and architectural brilliance.

Goals:

  • Highlight the scale and layout of the new Bangalore office through seamless drone storytelling
  • Deliver high-engagement visual content for internal and external showcase
  • Use FPV drone dynamics to capture angles and transitions that traditional cameras can’t
  • Create a standout asset that reflects Google’s innovative spirit and bold identity
